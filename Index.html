Biography

Jose Rizal was born on June 19, 1861, in Calamba, Laguna. He was a polymath, nationalist, and writer whose works inspired the Philippine revolution. He traveled extensively to study and spread his ideas.

Notable Works

  • Noli Me Tangere - A novel exposing the abuses of the Spanish clergy.
  • El Filibusterismo - A sequel advocating political reform and justice.
  • Various poems, essays, and letters showcasing his patriotism and love for the Filipino people.

Legacy

Rizal's martyrdom on December 30, 1896, in Bagumbayan (now Rizal Park) cemented his place as a national hero. His ideals for freedom and independence continue to inspire generations of Filipinos.
